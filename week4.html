Week 4 was the first time I actually touched p5.js. For User Input workshop, I still had minor mistakes and the code didn't work as expected at first. I was impressive with making white line follow cursor, code white circle as cursor run, and the most fun with random circles in random colours at a low framerate, so I included all of them here. It looked like confetti exploding slowly, a magic on your computer screen. I also looked at early generative artists like Vera Molnár and Georg Nees, people who were writing algorithms to make art in the 1960s, before most people even had computers. Now try drawing something!
